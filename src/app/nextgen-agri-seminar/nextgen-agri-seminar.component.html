Smart Crop Monitoring

Connected irrigation and nutrient-distribution equipment uses sensor data and imagery analysis to optimise resource usage and crop growth through real-time, precise, location-dependent adjustments.

Drone Farming

Drone surveillance and remote interventions based on image analysis and connected sensors communicating data with the drone aim to provide more frequent, cost-effective remote monitoring of large areas and enable remote interventions to boost yield and reduce pest losses while optimising deployment costs.

Smart-Livestock Monitoring

Individualised feeding-and-care plans based on connected-body sensor data and movement tracking to detect illnesses early and give each animal the best feed and medicine mix for growth.

Autonomous Farming Machinery

Self-operated machinery and robots that use connected-sensor data, GPS data, and imagery analysis to optimise resource utilisation, reduce labour, and increase yield.
