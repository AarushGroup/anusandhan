About the Entrepreneur

Andhra Pradesh has strong presence of Defence Public Sector Units. The state is home to the Eastern Naval Command in Visakhapatnam and Satish Dhawan Space Centre SHAR (SDSC SHAR), at the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO) in Sriharikota, the primary spaceport of India. The Hindustan Shipyard, founded in 1941, is a PSU that is focused on ship building, ship repair including major overhauls of Indian Navy submarines

Sector Highlights & Key Initiatives

  • Some of the key investments under active implementation in the Aerospace & Defence sector are as follows:
    • BEL’s Defence Systems Integration Complex for Weapons and Radars in Anantapur District
    • BEL’s New Generation Electro-optics & Advance Night Vision Cameras in Krishna District
    • Air Cargo Complex near Visakhapatnam
    • Stumpp Schuele Casings’s (SSC) upcoming Ammunitions manufacturing unit in Anantapur District
    • DRDO’s Missile Testing facility planned in Krishna District
    • Premier Explosive’s upcoming Solid Rocket Propellant Plant in Nellore District
    • World-class Maintenance, Repair and Overhaul (MRO) facility as a part of the proposed Bhogapuram Aerotropolis near Visakhapatnam
  • Andhra Pradesh has dedicated infrastructure facilities for Aerospace & Defence such DRDO’s Electronic Warfare Test Range including a design, development, production, and assembly unit in Kurnool District.
  • The State is blessed with Heavy Mineral Beach Sand (HMBS) a source for minerals such as Ilmenite and metal-grade Titanium, ideal for Aerospace & Defence manufacturing products.
